🏔️ EXPEDITION MANUAL

"Precision is the difference between a podium finish and a mountain rescue."

1. The Mission Objective

Your goal is to navigate 9 distinct legs of the Alpine Tour. You begin at the base and must reach the Oak Forest summit. Each leg requires a specific number of Correct Answers to move your bike from the starting point to the Safe Station.

2. Resource Management

You must monitor your dashboard constantly. Every action has a cost:

  • ⚡ ENERGY: Consumed with every question. If you hit 0%, you collapse from exhaustion.
  • 🚲 BIKE CONDITION: Your mechanical status. Wears down over time.
  • ⚠️ MALFUNCTIONS: If you answer incorrectly, you suffer a random mechanical failure (e.g., Broken Spokes, Chain Snap). This causes massive damage to your meters.

3. The Navigation System

Use the 🗺️ SHOW/HIDE MAP button to track your physical progress. Your pin moves in real-time as you earn points.

Note: You cannot see the next leg's terrain until you successfully reach the current checkpoint and save your progress.

4. Question Protocols

Multiple Choice Select the best grammatical fit to maintain speed.
Scrambled Sentences Type the words in the exact correct order to clear the path.
Fill the Blanks Type the correct verb conjugation to repair your momentum.

5. Checkpoints & Save Codes

When you reach a Safe Station (Cabin), you will receive:

  • 🏆 A Merit Badge: Added to your permanent Trophy Room.
  • 💾 A Save Code: A unique 4-8 character string. Write this down! Without this code, the mountain winds will reset your progress to Leg 1.

🚨 CRITICAL FAILURE WARNING

If Energy or Bike Condition hit 0%, the leg is failed. You will be air-lifted back to the start of that specific leg to try again.
