👩‍⚕️ HEALTH VOCABULARY - STARTERS & A1 👨‍⚕️

Doctor 🔊
/ˈdɒk.tər/
👨‍⚕️
A person who helps sick people.
Example: The doctor gave me medicine.
Nurse 🔊
/nɜːs/
👩‍⚕️
A person who works with the doctor.
Example: The kind nurse checked my temperature.
Hospital 🔊
/ˈhɒs.pɪ.təl/
🏥
A big place for sick people.
Example: We went to the hospital to see the doctor.
Sick 🔊
/sɪk/
🤒
When you do not feel well.
Example: I am sick today, so I can't go to school.
Hurt 🔊
/hɜːt/
🩹
To feel pain.
Example: I fell down and my knee hurts.
Medicine 🔊
/ˈmed.ɪ.sən/
💊
You take this to feel better.
Example: The sweet medicine helped my tummy.
Sleep 🔊
/sliːp/
🛌
To close your eyes and rest in bed.
Example: You need to sleep a lot when you are sick.
Head 🔊
/hed/
🤕
The top part of your body.
Example: I have a pain in my head.
Tummy 🔊
/ˈtʌm.i/
🤢
Your stomach.
Example: Eating too much candy gives you a tummy ache.
Tooth 🔊
/tuːθ/
🦷
The white things in your mouth to chew.
Example: Brush your tooth so it does not hurt.
Ear 🔊
/ɪər/
👂
You use this to hear sounds.
Example: The loud music hurts my ear.
Eye 🔊
/aɪ/
👁️
You use this to see things.
Example: My eye is red because I am sleepy.
Arm 🔊
/ɑːm/
💪
The long part of your body with a hand.
Example: The nurse put a bandage on my arm.
Leg 🔊
/leɡ/
🦵
The part of your body you use to walk.
Example: I hurt my leg when I was playing football.
Foot 🔊
/fʊt/
🦶
The part at the bottom of your leg.
Example: I have a shoe on my foot.
Hand 🔊
/hænd/
🖐️
The part of your body with five fingers.
Example: Wash your hand before you eat!
