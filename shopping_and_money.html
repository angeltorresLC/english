Money & Shopping!

Welcome! Let's master our B1 vocabulary and idioms.

🪙

Coin

Noun

A flat piece of metal used as money.

💴

Currency

Noun

The type of money used by a country (like Dollars or Yen!).

💰

To Afford

Verb

To have enough money to buy something.

🛍️

Bargain

Noun

An item bought at a much lower price than usual.

🤲

Change

Noun

The money returned to you when you pay more than the item costs.

💳

Account

Noun

An arrangement where a bank keeps your money safe.

⚖️

Balance

Noun

The sum of money that is left in your bank account.

😥

Debt

Noun

An amount of money that you owe to someone.

🙏

To Owe

Verb

To have the responsibility to pay someone back.

💎

Luxury

Noun

Something very expensive that brings great comfort and pleasure.

🛒

Checkout

Noun

The place in a supermarket where you pay for your items.

🏷️

Discount

Noun

The amount of money reduced from the usual price.

🧸

Inexpensive

Adjective

Having a reasonable price; not costing a lot.

🎁

To Donate

Verb

To freely give goods or money to an organization to help others.

🔙

To Return

Verb

To bring back an item to the store to get a refund.

🏥💸

Cost an arm and a leg

Idiom

When something is incredibly expensive!

🪟👀

Window shopping

Idiom

Looking at goods in store windows without intending to buy them.

🌧️🐖

Save for a rainy day

Idiom

To save your money for a time in the future when you might need it.

🛍️😵

Shop till you drop

Idiom

To go shopping for a very long time until you are completely exhausted.
